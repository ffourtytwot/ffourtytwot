Welcome to Mstislavl!

Mstislavl is one of the oldest cities in Belarus, with a rich history dating back to the 12th century. Located in the Mogilev region, it offers a unique blend of historical landmarks, cultural experiences, and natural beauty.

Learn more about tourism

Key Highlights

Mstislavl Castle Hill

The Castle Hill is the historical heart of Mstislavl. It offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape and features remnants of the ancient Mstislavl fortress.

Alexander Nevsky Church

The Alexander Nevsky Church is a beautiful example of 19th-century Orthodox architecture. Its elegant design and serene atmosphere make it a must-see.

Knights' Festival

Mstislavl hosts an annual Knights' Festival, a vibrant event that brings history to life with medieval reenactments, crafts, and performances.
